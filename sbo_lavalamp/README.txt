Lava Lamps (lavalamp) mod for Skyblock: Zero


by thefamilygrog66
modified and stipped by F3mboyPhoenix

Description:
Coloured Lava Lamps, loosely based on Tonyka's wall torches from the 3dforniture/homedecor mod.

After placing a lava lamp, the player can turn it off/on again by right-clicking on it.
